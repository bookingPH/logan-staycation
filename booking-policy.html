Booking Policy

Please read before making a reservation.

Reservation Fee

A non-refundable reservation fee is required to confirm your booking. This fee will be deducted from your total balance upon check-in.

How to Reserve

  • Select your preferred room and dates on the booking page
  • Fill in your guest details
  • Choose any add-ons you want
  • Send the reservation fee via GCash, PayMaya, or bank transfer
  • Upload your payment screenshot
  • Wait for confirmation via email (usually within 1–2 hours)

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out time: 12:00 PM (noon)
  • Early check-in or late check-out may be accommodated subject to availability

House Rules

  • No smoking inside the property
  • Quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM
  • Pets are only allowed in pet-friendly rooms (additional fee applies)
  • Guests are responsible for any damages to the property

Payment Methods

  • GCash
  • PayMaya
  • Bank Transfer

Cash payments upon check-in are accepted for the remaining balance only.

Confirmation

Bookings are confirmed only after your payment screenshot has been verified. You will receive a confirmation email within 1–2 hours.
