Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. Here's what you need to know.

Reservation Fee

The reservation fee paid to hold your dates is non-refundable under all circumstances.

Cancellation by Guest

  • More than 7 days before check-in: Full refund of any balance paid beyond the reservation fee
  • 3–7 days before check-in: 50% refund of any balance paid beyond the reservation fee
  • Less than 3 days before check-in: No refund
  • No-show: No refund

How to Cancel

To cancel your booking, contact us directly via phone or the email you received in your confirmation. Please provide your booking reference number.

Rescheduling

We allow free rescheduling if requested at least 7 days before your original check-in date, subject to room availability.

Cancellation by the Property

In the rare event that we need to cancel your booking, you will receive a full refund including the reservation fee.

Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days via the same payment method used to book.
